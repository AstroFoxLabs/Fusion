# Fusion

Fusion is an Electron Desktop Application, that makes merging/combining images as easy as never before.
There is no limit on how many images you can combine with eachother and to make it easier, drag and drop is enabled for all images.
Export them easily as a batch with a single click or just export a single.

## Features

**Merge images:** Merge images together and name them. Exporting as a batch is easily possible.

https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/a6db4713-ef18-4bce-ae90-0a8d3374a012

**Drag and Drop**: Drag and drop your images to easily decide the position of the image in the merge process.

https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/65e2da76-29b9-4a02-bb13-c75109eec4e2


**Merge multiple together**: Instead of just two images into one, you can also merge 5, 10 or 20 together. How many you want.
